What is in North Europe?

The coldest region of the continent with the Arctic circle encompassing portions of it, some of the population centers here either experience long days with sunlight shining down the majority of the day or long nights with seldom any sunlight during the day. The current de facto language for international relations on the planet, English, originated from this region, too.
