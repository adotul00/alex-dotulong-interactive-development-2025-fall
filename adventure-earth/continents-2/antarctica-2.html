Quick facts about Antarctica

This is the coldest continent on the planet located on the southernmost part of the planet. Due to the conditions on this continent, there are no permanent human settlements. Some countries have established research bases here, however.

Some notable fauna on this continent include:

  • Emperor penguins
  • A variety of petrel birds
  • Elephant seals
  • Leopard seals
