Quick facts about Europe

This continent is infamous for being in control over almost the entire population of the planet at one point in human history during their so-called "Age of Exploration." Many of the countries across the planet have been influenced at some point or another by at least one of the countries in Europe, and the formation of current country borders can be attributed to that influence.
