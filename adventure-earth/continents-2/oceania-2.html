Quick facts about Oceania

Like its name, there is a lot of ocean in this area of the planet. Most of these countries are made up of small islands and islets and are also a popular destination for tourists. The largest land mass on this continent is Australia, which is also shares responsibility with New Zealand to protect these small island countries due to their lack of size and resources to defend themselves. Because of rising sea levels, most of these countries also risk being completely inundated by the ocean.
