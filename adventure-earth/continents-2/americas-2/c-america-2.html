What is in Central America?

This region contains most of the smallest main-land countries in the Americas. The beautiful weather is paired with a beautiful landscape. It is also one of the most important regions regarding intercontinental trade with the panama canal connecting the Atlantic and Pacific oceans.

List of countries:

  • Belize
  • Costa Rica
  • El Salvador
  • Guatemala
  • Honduras
  • Nicaragua
  • Panama
