What is in South Europe?

Being mostly surrounded by the Mediterranean Sea to its south and the North Atlantic Ocean to the west, this region boasts a large coastline with perfect weather to sit at a beach and relax. Also due to its location, it was known to be where the majority of trade happened, especially after the humans invented ways to traverse over the water. The center of the Catholic religion is also located here, specifically at the Vatican City.
