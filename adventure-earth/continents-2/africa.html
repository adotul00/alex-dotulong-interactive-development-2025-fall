Quick facts about Africa

Africa is home to the vast Sahara Desert accompanied with a lush rainforest further south. Rich in resources, this continent has been taken advantage of by European powers for centuries and even sees some external geopolitical forces to this day. The former colonial powers came, drew borders that did not conform to the way the communities functioned, and left without ensuring that the humans living there were well-equipped to run these "new countries" that had been formed without their consent. This caused for many of the countries here to struggle even after independence.
