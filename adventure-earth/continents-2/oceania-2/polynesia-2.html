What is in Polynesia?

This region covers the most ocean compared to the others but contains mostly islands that are controlled by other countries outside of the continent with a few by either Australia and New Zealand. Its name also means "many islands" because of the sheer number of islands here.

List of countries:

  • Samoa
  • Tonga
  • Tuvalu

Some notable flora:

  • Teuila
  • Pandanus
  • Tiare
  • Frangipani

Some notable fauna:

  • Pygmy Sperm Whale
  • Polynesian Triller
  • Spinner Dolphin
  • Blue Moon Butterfly
