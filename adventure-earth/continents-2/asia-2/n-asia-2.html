What is in North Asia?

This region is not only the coldest region in the continent but also has the coldest permanently inhabited areas on the planet. There is also only one country that makes up this region because of its vast size, Russia. Most of its population on this side are also mostly made up of indigenous people and tribes to their respective areas. It is also the least densely populated region of Asia.
