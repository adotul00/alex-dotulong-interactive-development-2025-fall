What is in South America?

In the heart of the continent lies the planet's largest rainforest and river system, the Amazon. Many flora and fauna call this place home including small pockets of human settlements that live more primitively compared to the larger and more condensed populations closer to the coast. Towards the southwestern part of the continent, a mountain range called the Andes towers alongside the coast.
