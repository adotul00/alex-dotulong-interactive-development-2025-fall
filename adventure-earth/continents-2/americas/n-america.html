What is in North America?

Spanning from the frigid Arctic circle to the tropics in its southern point, this region is home to a handful of large countries and many creatures and plants.

List of countries:

  • Canada
  • Greenland (Denmark)
  • Mexico
  • United States of America

Some notable flora:

  • Flowering dogwood
  • Redwood trees
  • Maple trees
  • Cacti

Fauna found here:

  • Bison
  • Grizzly bear
  • Moose
  • Manatee
