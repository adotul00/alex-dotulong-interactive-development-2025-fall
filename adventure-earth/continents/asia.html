Quick facts about Asia

This continent is the most populated continent on the planet, having approximately 60% of the planet’s human population. The majority of the planet’s manufacturing happens here, especially with the amount of humans living here and the low cost to manufacture products compared to other places on the planet. It is also abundant in resources, so it does not need to spend nearly as much as other parts of the world to attain them.
