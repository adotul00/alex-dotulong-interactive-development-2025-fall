What is in Melanesia?

Most of the islands in this region are larger, and its name means "black islands." This is most likely because of the skin color of the Melanesian people. The western half of the island of New Guinea is controlled by Indonesia, but because the political country boundaries is often used over cultural boundaries, this island is split between Asia and Oceania.
