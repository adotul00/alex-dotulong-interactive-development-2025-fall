What is in Central Africa?

From the arid Sahara Desert in its northern area to the dense jungles of the Congo, the heart of the African continent is challenged by a large variety of issues. Diamonds are one of the most sought after resourse in the area and has been a difficult stain on this region’s past, especially with the brutal way its colonizers treated the locals. When it comes to traversing the jungles in the south, it often takes a very long time since the creation of permanent infrastructure is difficult and very expensive.
