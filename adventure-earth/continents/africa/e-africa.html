What is in East Africa?

This region is located at one of the most important places in global shipping trade with the strait between Djibouti and Yemen being the narrow point connecting the Red Sea with the Indian Ocean. This valuable trading route also increased the rate of piracy, most notably the Somali pirates on its easternmost point. East Africa also comprises of many other island nations that are often forgotten about with the exception of Madagascar.
