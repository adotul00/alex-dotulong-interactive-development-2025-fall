What is in West Africa?

A primary stopping point for many European ships on the way to Asia by ship during the colonial period, ensuring that each of the respective European powers had access to ports and the resources in the area was a vital thing. Two major European powers controlled the majority of this region, and it struggles with territory disputes to this day considering the nomadic nature of the humans that lived across the Sahara Desert. Africa’s most populous country is in this region, namely Nigeria.
