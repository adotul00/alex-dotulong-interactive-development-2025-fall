The Outer Core

Interesting facts about the outer core

Ranging from about 7,000°F to almost 11,000°F, this hot mixture of metals circulates around the inner core of the planet. This constant flow provides the planet its magnetic field, offering it protection from the solar wind coming from the sun.

The outer core is made up of iron, nickel, sulfur, and oxygen.
